# PlumWallPaper · Visual Benchmarks (v12.1 Expert)
> Updated: 2026-05-04

## 🏆 Competitor DNA Analysis

1. **WaifuX (The "Immersive" Standard)**:
   - **Key Visual**: Borderless large-scale cards with "floating" buttons.
   - **Material**: Ultra-thin glass, heavy use of backdrop-filter.
   - **Architecture**: Content-first, sidebar is often hidden or minimal icon-rail.

2. **iWallpaper / Dynamic Wallpaper (The "Utility" Standard)**:
   - **Key Visual**: High density grid with explicit labels and status badges (Retina/FPS).
   - **Architecture**: Classic macOS Sidebar + Header segmented control.
   - **Material**: Standard `systemSource` material.

3. **Apple System Settings (The "Refined" Standard)**:
   - **Key Visual**: Perfect spacing (12px-24px), thick-weighted "SF Pro Display" headers.
   - **Focus**: Extreme legibility and accessibility.

## ⚠️ Self-Critique of v1 Prototype
- Too "Default": Felt like a standard settings menu, not a "Best" creative tool.
- Flat: Lacked the Z-axis depth (Spatial UI) needed for a premium 2026 app.
- Static: Wallpaper cards didn't "breathe" or show dynamic shaders.
